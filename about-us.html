What are Guerrilla Allotments?

Welcome to Guerrilla Allotments, where urban gardening meets social impact!
We're on a mission to transform unused spaces in Glasgow into thriving allotments, providing you with the opportunity to grow your own fresh produce while making a difference in our community.
For a small monthly fee, you can rent a plot and join our green-fingered revolution. Join Guerrilla Allotments today and become part of a community that's cultivating change, one plot at a time. Together, we can sow the seeds of a greener, more compassionate Glasgow.

But that's not all! Our commitment to fighting food insecurity goes beyond the garden. All profits from our allotment rentals are donated directly to local food banks, ensuring that your green thumb helps nourish those in need. And if your plot yields more than you can handle, we've got you covered.
Surplus produce is either donated to food banks or sold at our unofficial market near the allotment area, further supporting our cause.
